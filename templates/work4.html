004 : Digital Car Parking System

The digital car parking system offers improved user interface. Moreover, with ticketless and cashless parking, the users don't need to carry small parking tickets which are prone to lose.

This project was implemented during 2nd year of my engineering.
In this project I collaborated with IT team to develop and execute a successful parking system strategy and created "Digital Car Parking System" in which the user can easily browse for vacant parking slots throughout the parking area by just scrolling through his/her fingers in mobile device.
